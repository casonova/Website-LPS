Intership Program



   The LPS Social Awareness Internship Programme (SAIP) provides opportunities
    for students to work with different organisations involved in social sectors, such as
    health, education and the environment. LPS students undertake summer internships
    in many not-for-profit organisations such as CARE Foundation, WWF-Pakistan and
    media groups such as Dawn in Pakistan. Internships not only help students take
    their first steps in the professional arena but also assist with university placement
    and admissions. In 2017, around 1500 LPS students interned at various organisations
